## ðŸš¨ Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Financial Risks

**1. High Customer Churn (>10%)**
- **Impact:** Reduce LTV by 40%
- **Mitigation:**
  - Improve onboarding
  - Add sticky features (integrations)
  - Annual contracts with discounts
  - Proactive customer success

**2. CAC Inflation (paid channels become expensive)**
- **Impact:** LTV:CAC drops below 5x
- **Mitigation:**
  - Double down on organic (SEO, content)
  - Build referral program
  - Focus on product-led growth
  - Optimize conversion funnel

**3. Infrastructure Costs Spike**
- **Impact:** Margins drop from 85% to 70%
- **Mitigation:**
  - Negotiate volume discounts
  - Optimize scraping efficiency
  - Use cheaper proxy providers
  - Implement smart caching

**4. Competition Enters Market**
- **Impact:** Pricing pressure, customer loss
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build moat (integrations, data network effects)
  - Focus on specific niches
  - Superior customer service
  - Fast feature iteration
